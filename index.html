Recommendations

Data-Backed Findings

REC-01Adjust Orange Line headways during 7-9am peak to reduce platform crowding delays-18% delays
REC-02Implement dynamic signal priority for Green Line surface segments B and E branches-14% delays
REC-03Rebalance Red Line train frequency Ashmont vs Braintree branch splits at peak hours+12% capacity
REC-04Deploy Silver Line bus bunching detection with real-time dispatch correction-22% bunching
REC-05Optimize Commuter Rail inbound scheduling to reduce North and South Station crowding$0.8M savings
REC-06Establish system-wide real-time delay monitoring dashboard for operations center$1.6M savings
